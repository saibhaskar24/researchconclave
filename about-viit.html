About Research Conclave

The management landscape, globally, is undergoing transformation in view of increasing adoption of artificial intelligence, machine learning, internet of things, industry 4.0, robotic process automation, fintech, block chain, analytics and so forth. Data can now be gathered, processed at a faster rate which in turn impacts productivity and ultimately the competitive advantage. This changing landscape requires management to integrate business technology and replace redundant tasks with reengineered business processes. The increasing intelligence impacts products and production systems, their intra-company cross-linking and their cross-company integration into value creation networks. Appreciating this, GL Bajaj Institute of Management and Research is pleased to announce it’s first Research Conclave on “Industry 4.0: Management Challenges and Opportunities” on Saturday February 22, 2020. This is a step towards understanding and liberating the management thought processes in this era of rapid changes and disruption. This research conclave is designed to bring together thought leaders in the area to deliberate on management strategy in light of the changing practices in various business domains. In addition, we also invite research papers in the areas of management change, strategy planning, sustainability etc and the best papers will compete for presentation and award.
