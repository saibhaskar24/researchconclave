RESEARCH CONCLAVE - 2020

About Research Conclave

The technology is undergoing transformation in view of increasing adoption of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and internet of things, robotic process automation, 5G wireless communications and so forth. The increasing intelligence impacts the human life. This research conclave is designed to bring together leaders in the area of modern technology and sharing the ideas. In addition, we also invite research papers in the areas of machine learning, IoT, communication etc.

About College

Vignan’s Institute of Information Technology was established in the year 2002 by the great Visionary Dr. L. Rathaiah. This is a co-education self-financing Engineering college approved by the AICTE and affiliated to JNTU-Kakinada & located in Visakhapatnam, Steel City of Andhra Pradesh. Vignan’s IIT provides its learning community, state-of-art facilities, infrastructure and competent faculty. The Institute encourages student fraternity to participate in various technical & cultural activities so that they can grow as all-rounder.
