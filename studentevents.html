V-IGNITE-2.0

V-IGNITE 2.0 is a nationwide initiative to provide students a platform to solve some of the pressing problems we face in our daily lives, and thus inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem solving. Last year 350+ teams and 2000 students participated throughout Pan India

In the ever growing world of technology, we go to extreme lengths to achieve solutions to our daily problems hence making life easier for us. But there are some issues out there in the real world that needs attention more than we realize. These problems are left behind while the world moves towards technological advancement. Thus to solve these kind of real world problems, through the help of technology; we propose this hackathon, V-Ignite, that will let all the aspiring and creative budding computer engineers to come forward with their innovative solutions to these problems.

(The website will be available 3-4 days prior to the commencement of the hackathon so as to initiate the countdown and give prior knowledge regarding this hackathon to the participants.)

Each team can get registered once the website is available or after the hackathon commences. The teams should consist of minimum of 2 or maximum of 4 members. There will be 3 rounds, wherein the teams can participate in the first 2 rounds from their place but the 3rd final round will take place at our college Vignan’s Institute of Information Technology, Duvvada.

The V-Ignite hackathon will take place on Feb 26th-27th 2021
ROUND 1: IDEATION PHASE
Hackathon commences on Jan 1st 2021

Round 1: From 1st Jan 26th Jan 2021

In this phase, the participants can get registered as a team of 2-4 members and submit their ideas on any domain of their choice. Some of the real world problem domains are- Education, Health, Agriculture, Finance, Natural Resources, etc. The teams are expected to submit their ideas in a neat and orderly format. It can be either a PDF, word, or presentation format. The following are some key points that the submissions must contain-

  • • Domain selected.
  • • What is the Problem Statement?
  • • What is the Problem Statement?
  • • Why has the problem not been solved till date?
  • • Approach taken towards this problem?
  • • Solution
  • • Technology Stack

All the teams must submit their ideas on or before August 2nd.
Once the Round 1 ends on 26th Jan 2021, our team will get back to them with their result by Jan 30th 2021


ROUND 2: PROTOTYPE PHASE

Round 2:1st Feb to 10th Feb 2021

In this round, the teams that have been selected after Round -1, will have to get ready with a prototype to display what their idea wants to depict through Telephonic or skype Interaction.
This prototype can be a short demo of how their end product will actually work and help the end user of their chosen domain.

The team should have a thorough knowledge and should be ready for any questions that may be asked.After Round 2 ends, the teams will be shortlisted to 25-28 teams as the finalists of the V-IGNITE2.0HACKATHON. The teams will be notified of the result by 10th Feb 2021 and can start working on their project or make further preparations for the final round. The teams will also need to reply back to us to confirm whether they will attend the final round or not.

The selected teams will need to report to Vignan’s Institute of Information Technology, for the final round of the hackathon to implement their project.

ROUND 3: FINAL IMPLEMENTATION PHASE

A complete 24 hour Hackathon that,
Starts on 10.00A.M. of Feb 26th
Ends on 4.00P.M. of Feb 27th
The teams will be allotted their respective tables and it is mandatory that at least 1 team member should always be present at their table.

During this 24 hours HACKATHON;

  •  Judging round 1 will take place on Feb 26th around 3:30 p.m. - 5:30p.m.
  •  Judging round 2 will take place once the hackathon ends which is from 12.30 P.M..-2.30 P.M.
  •  Based on the above judging rounds, the Top 10 teams from the finalists will be selected to give a presentation of their project.

The marks distribution is as follows

Judging Round 1 – 25%

Judging Round 2 – 50%

Presentation Round – 25%

The Judges will be judging the teams and projects based on some key factors like –

• What is the problem?

• How is the team solving the problem?

• Use of efficient and effective technology.

• How is it different than other solutions?

• Who are the targeted end users?

Please note-

• Accommodation will not be provided for teams coming from other states due to COVID.

• Other State Teams can participate the event through Online

• Food will be provided only during the hackathon which is from 26th Feb (Lunch, Dinner) and 27th

Feb(Breakfast and lunch).

• Evening snacks and Midnight snacks will be provided as well during the hackathon.

• WIFI will be provided to the teams for doing their project. Better to bring own internet dongles for faster access.

• The teams will be judged on various criteria as mentioned above and the team who has implemented their solution in the best way will be considered.
