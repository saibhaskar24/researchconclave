About Research Conclave

The technology is undergoing transformation in view of increasing adoption of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and internet of things, robotic process automation, 5G wireless communications and so forth. The increasing intelligence impacts the human life. This research conclave is designed to bring together leaders in the area of modern technology and sharing the ideas. In addition, we also invite research papers in the areas of machine learning, IoT, communication etc.

Objective of the Conclave

  • Showcasing on the case of research activities by the faculty members and other institute research scholars and faculty members.
  • Providing a common platform for researcher and academician.
  • Inspiring and encouraging research scholars to demonstrate their innovative research ideas and thought.
