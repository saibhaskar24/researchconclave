About Research Conclave

The technology is undergoing transformation in view of increasing adoption of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Internet of Things, Robotic Process Automation, 5G Wireless Communications and so forth. The increasing intelligence impacts the human life. This research conclave is designed to bring together leaders in the area of modern technology and sharing the ideas. In addition, we also invite research papers in the areas of Machine Learning, IoT, Wireless Communication etc.

Objective of the Conclave

  • Showcasing research activities and capabilities by the internal faculty members and external research scholars and faculty members.
  • Providing a common platform for researchers and academicians.
  • Inspiring, encouraging, and mentoring research scholars to demonstrate their innovative research ideas and thoughts.
